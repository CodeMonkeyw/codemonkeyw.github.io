ya-namp brings the look and feel of the classic Winamp music player to your modern Yandex Music library. You get the nostalgic visual experience of the late 90s combined with the large library access of contemporary streaming services.

✨ Key Features

  • Classic interface design based on the original Winamp layout.
  • Full access to your complete Yandex Music collection.
  • Lightweight performance that runs inside your browser.
  • Support for custom visual themes and skins.
  • Simple playback controls for queues and playlists.

💻 System Requirements

To use this player, make sure your computer meets these basic needs:

  • Windows 10 or Windows 11 operating system.
  • A modern web browser like Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge, or Mozilla Firefox.
  • Active internet connection to stream music from Yandex.
  • Audio output device such as speakers or headphones.

🚀 How to Start

  1. Open your preferred web browser.

  3. Look for the release area on the right side of the screen.
  4. Click the latest version link to visit the download page.

Once you open the software, you must link your account to play music. Click the settings icon in the main window. Select the login option. A secure window will appear. Enter your Yandex credentials to authorize the connection. The player will sync your existing playlists automatically. You can toggle between different skins using the right-click menu on the background border.
